“rolling on or forward” (opp. vivaṭṭa “rolling back”), with ref. to the development of the Universe & time (kappa) the *ascending* aeon (vivaṭṭa the *descending* cycle), evolution Iti.99; Pp.60; Vism.419; Sdhp.484, Sdhp.485. *\-vivaṭṭa* a period within which evolution & dissolution of the world takes place, a complete world\-cycle (see also; *[vivaṭṭa](vivaṭṭa.md)* DN.i.14; AN.ii.142; Iti.15, Iti.99; Pp.60.)
