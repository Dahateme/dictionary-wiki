1. expansion, breadth; instr. *vitthārena* in breadth Mil.17; same abl. *[vitthārato](vitthārato.md)* Ja.i.49
2. extension, detail; often in C. style, introducing detailed explanation of the subject in question, either with simple statement “*vitthāro*” (i.e. here the foll detail; opp. *[saṅkhepa](saṅkhepa.md)*), e.g. DN\-a.i.65, DN\-a.i.229; Snp\-a.325 [cp. same in BSk. “vistaraḥ,” e.g. Divy.428], or with compounds *\-kathā* Snp\-a.464; Pv\-a.19; *\-desanā* Snp\-a.163 *\-vacana* Snp\-a.416. Thus in general often in instr. or abl. as adv. “in detail,” in extenso (opp. sankhittena in short): *vitthārena* DN.iii.241; SN.iv.93; AN.ii.77, AN.ii.177 AN.ii.189; AN.iii.177; Pp.41; Pv\-a.53, Pv\-a.113; *[vitthārato](vitthārato.md)* Vism.351, Vism.479; Pv\-a.71, Pv\-a.77, Pv\-a.81. Cp. similarly BSk. vistarena kāryaṃ Divy.377.
