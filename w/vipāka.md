fruit, fruition, product; always in pregnant meaning of “result, effect, consequence (of one’s action),” either as good & meritorious (*[kusala](kusala.md)* or bad & detrimental (*[akusala](akusala.md)*). Hence “retribution (*kamma˚*;), reward or punishment. See on term e.g. *Dhs. translation* introd.2xciii; *Cpd.* 43. 249
