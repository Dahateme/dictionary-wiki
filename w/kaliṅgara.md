(BB ḷ)

2. a plank, viz. a step in a staircase, in sopāna˚ Vin.ii.128, cp. sopāna\-kaḷevara.

* *\-ūpadhāna* a wooden block used for putting one’s head on when sleeping SN.ii.267; Mil.366;
