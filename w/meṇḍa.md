dial., cp. Prk. měṇṭha & miṇṭha: Pischel, *Prk. Gr.* § 293. The Dhtm (156) gives a root *meṇḍ* (meḍ) in meaning of “koṭilla,” i.e. crookedness. The Ved (Sk.) word for ram is meṣa
