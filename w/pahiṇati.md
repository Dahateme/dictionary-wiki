pp *pahita2* (q.v.). There is another aor. *[pāhesi](pāhesi.md)* (Sk. prāhaiṣīt) in analogy to which a new pres. *[pāheti](pāheti.md)* has been formed, so that pāhesi is now felt to be a der. fr. pāheti & accordingly is grouped with the latter. All other forms with he˚ (pahetuṃ e.g.) are to be found under pāheti.
