adjective noun one who marks cattle, i.e. one who castrates or deprives of virility Ja.iv.364 (spelt *[tilañchaka](tilañchaka.md)* in text, but right in variant reading), explained as “tisūlâdi\-ankakaraṇena lañchakā ca lakkhaṇakārakā ti attho” (p 366). cp. nillacchita.

cp. Sk. nirlāñchana, of nirlāñchayati = nis \+ laccheti
