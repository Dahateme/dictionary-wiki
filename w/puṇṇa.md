full seldom by itself (only passage so far pannarase puṇṇāya puṇṇamāya rattiyā DN.i.47 = Snp.p.139). nor\-(only Snp.835 muttakarīsa˚), usually in compounds, and there mostly restricted to phrases relating to the full moon.

* *\-[ghaṭa](ghaṭa.md)* a full pitcher (for feeding the bhikkhus, as offering on festive days, cp. *J.P.T.S.* 1884) Dhp\-a.i.147 Kp\-a.118 (variant reading suvaṇṇaghaṭa); DN\-a.i.140 (˚paṭimaṇḍita ghara).
* *\-[canda](canda.md)* the full moon Ja.i.149, Ja.i.267; Ja.v.215
* *\-[patta](patta.md)* a full bowl (as gift, ˚ṃ deti to give an ample gift Ja.iii.535.
* *\-[baddha](baddha.md)* at Mil.191 should be read as ˚bhadda.
* *\-[bala](bala.md)* at DN\-a.i.110 read puñña\-bala.
* *\-[bhadda](bhadda.md)* worshipper of Puṇṇabhadda, perhaps a Yakkha (father of the Yakkha Harikesa) Mnd.92 (Vāsuvadeva, Baladeva P. and Maṇibhadda, cp. p. 89); Mil.191 (pisācā maṇibhaddā p.).
* *\-[mā](mā.md)* the full moon (night) DN.i.47 (komudiyā cātumāsiniyā puṇṇāya puṇṇamāya rattiyā cp. DN\-a.i.140); Snp.p.139 (similar); MN.iii.21; Ja.v.215 (dve p\-māyo); Vism.292 (puṇṇa\-m\-uposatha = puṇṇa\-māuposatha), Vism.418 (Phagguṇa\-puṇṇama\-divase); Vv\-a.66 (āsāḷhi p.); Pv\-a.137 (id.); DN\-a.i.140; Dhp\-a.iii.461 (komudi).
* *\-māsa = ˚mā* only in loc. puṇṇamāse Vv.81#1 (= *puṇṇa\-māsiyaṃ sukka\-pakkhe pannarasiyaṃ* Vv\-a.314; the similar pass. at Vv\-a.321 reads, prob. by mistake, sukka\-pakkha\-pāṭiyaṃ: see *[pāṭī](pāṭī.md)*); Ja.v.215 (= puṇṇa candāya rattiyā C.).
* *\-māsī* (f.; fr. *\-māsa) mā* Ja.i.86 (Phagguṇi p.); Vv\-a.314; cp. BSk. pūrṇamāsī Avs.i.182.

pp. of *pṛ*; Vedic pṛṇāti, Pass. pūryate, \*pelē to fill; cp. Sk. prāṇa & pūrṇa = Av. pərəna; Lith. pílnas Lat. plēnus; Goth fulls = E. full = Ger voll
