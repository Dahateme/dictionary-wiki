praise i.e. that which is commendable, profit, merit, advantage good result, blessing in or from (c. loc.). There are *five* ānisaṃsā sīlavato sīla\-sampadāya or blessings which accrue to the virtuous enumerated at DN.ii.86 viz. *[bhogakkhandha](bhogakkhandha.md)* great wealth, *[kittisadda](kittisadda.md)* good report *[visārada](visārada.md)* self\-confidence, *asammūlho kālaṃ karoti* an untroubled death, *saggaṃ lokaṃ uppajjati* a happy state after death
