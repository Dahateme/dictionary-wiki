*[lapanā](lapanā.md)* (f.)

1. talking, muttering; esp. prattling or uttering indistinct words for the sake of begging, patter DN.i.8; AN.ii.26; AN.iii.430; Mnd.389; Ne.94; Mil.383. As f. *[lapanā](lapanā.md)* at Vb.352; Vism.23 & Vism.27 (def.); Vb\-a.482.
2. the mouth, in cpd. *lapana\-ja* “mouth born,” i.e. tooth Ja.vi.218 (= mukhaja C.). Cp. *ālapana ālapanatā, ullapana*.
