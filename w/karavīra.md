1. the oleander, Nerium odorum. Its flower was used especially in garlands worn by delinquents (see *[kaṇṭha](kaṇṭha.md)*)
2. a kind of grass Ja.iv.92. *\-[patta](patta.md)* a kind of arrow MN.i.429.

cp. Sk. karavīra
