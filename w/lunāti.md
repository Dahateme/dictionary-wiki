to cut, cut off, mow, reap Mil.33 (yavalāvakā yavaṃ lunanti); Dhs\-a.39  
pp *lūna (& luta);* \-Caus I. *lāvayati* Mhvs.10, Mhvs.30; Caus. ii. *[lavāpeti](lavāpeti.md)* to cause to mow Vin.ii.180  
■ A Pass. *[lūyati](lūyati.md)* [fr. *lu* ] is found at DN.i.141 (aor. lūyiṃsu) and at corresponding passage Pp.56 (imper. lūyantu, where *[dubbā](dubbā.md)* is to be corrected to *dabbhā*)  
■ See lava, lavaka, lavana lāyati, lavati.

*lū*, given as *lu* at Dhtp.504 (“chedana”) & Dhtm.728 (“paccheda”). For etym. cp. Gr. λύω to loosen Lat. luo to pay a fine, Goth. fraliusan to lose; Ger. los E. lose & loose
