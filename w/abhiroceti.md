2. to please, satisfy, entertain gladden Vv.64#24 (but Vv\-a.292: abhibhavitvā vijjotati thus to no. 3).
3. variant reading for *atiroceti* (to surpass in splendour) at Vv.81#12, cp. also no. 2.
