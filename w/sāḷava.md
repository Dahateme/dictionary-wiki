certain dish, perhaps a kind of salad, given as “lambila,” i.e. bitter or astringent at Dhs\-a.320 (made of *[badara](badara.md)* or *[kapiṭṭha](kapiṭṭha.md)*); cp Vin.iv.259.

cp. Sk. ṣāḍava, which is given in diff. meaning, viz. “comfits with fruits”
