kind of plant, which is enumerated at Vin.iv.35 = DN\-a.i.81 as one of the aggabīja, i.e. plants propagated by slips or cuttings, together with ajjuka & hirivera. At Ja.vi.536 the C. gives *[bhūtanaka](bhūtanaka.md)* as expln. According to Childers it is the plant Samīraṇa.
