1. [Sk. anukaṛṣati] to draw after, to repeat, recite, quote DN.ii.255 (silokaṃ).
2. [Sk. *ava\-* kaṛṣati] to draw or take of, to remove, throw down, Thag.869 (aor. *[anvakāsi](anvakāsi.md)* = khipi, chaḍḍesi C.).
