certain measure of capacity, originally for grain; in older texts usually applied to a liquid measure (udaka˚). Its size is given by Bdhgh. at Snp\-a.476 as follows: “cattāro patthā āḷhakāni doṇaṃ etc.”\-udakāḷhaka SN.v.400; AN.ii.55 = AN.iii.337; Vv\-a.155  
■ In other connections at Ja.i.419 (aḍḍh˚); Ja.iii.541 (mitaṃ āḷhakena dhañña\-māpaka\-kammaṃ kataṃ C.); Mil.229 (patt˚); Dhp\-a.iii.367 (aḍḍh˚).

* *\-[thālikā](thālikā.md)* a bowl of the capacity of an āḷhaka Vin.i.240; AN.iii.369; Dhp\-a.iii.370 (variant reading bhatta\-thālikā).

Sk. āḍhaka, fr. \*āḍha probably meaning “grain”
