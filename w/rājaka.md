adjective (\-˚) characteristic of the king, king\-; in cpds *arājaka* without a king Ja.vi.39 (raṭṭhe); *[sarājaka](sarājaka.md)* including the king Tikp.26; f. *sarājikā* Vin.i.209 (parisā) Also in phrase *anikkhanta\-rājake* (loc. abs.) when the king has not gone out Vin.iv.160.

rāja \+ ka, the ending belonging to the whole cpd.
