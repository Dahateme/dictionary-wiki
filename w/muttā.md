pearl Vv.37#7 (˚ācita); Pv.ii.7#5 (\+ veḷuriya); Mhvs.30, Mhvs.66. Eight sorts of pearls are enumerated at Mvu. 11, 14, viz. haya\-gaja\-rath’ āmalakā valay’anguli\-veṭhakā kakudha\-phala\-pākatikā, i.e horse\-, elephant\-, waggon\-, myrobalan\-, bracelet\-, ring\-kakudha fruit\-, and common pearls.
