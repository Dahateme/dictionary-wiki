covetousness, greed. Defined at Vism.468 as “lubbhanti tena, sayaṃ vā lubbhati, lubbhana\-mattam eva vā taṃ,” with several comparisons following. Often found in triad of *lobha, dosa, moha* (greed, anger bewilderment, forming the three principles of demerit see *[kusala](kusala.md)*\-mūla), e.g. at AN.iv.96; Iti.83, Iti.84; Vism.116 Duka\-pa.9, Kp.18 sq.
