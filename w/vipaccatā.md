at Vin.ii.88 is perhaps a der. fr. vi \+ *vac*, and not *pac*, thus representing a Sk. *\*vivācyatā*, meaning “challenging in disputation,” quarrelsomeness, provocation See also *[vipāceti](vipāceti.md)*. If fr. vi \+ *pac*, the meaning would be something like “heatedness, exasperation.”
