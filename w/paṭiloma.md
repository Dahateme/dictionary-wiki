adjective “against the hair,” in reverse order, opposite, contrary, backward; usually combined with anuloma i.e. forward & backward Vin.i.1; AN.iv.448 etc (see *[paṭiccasamuppāda](paṭiccasamuppāda.md)*); Ja.ii.307. *\-pakkha* opposition Pv\-a.114 (cp. *[paṭipakkha](paṭipakkha.md)*).
