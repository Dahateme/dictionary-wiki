* *\-mattiyo* (pl.) of the size of a j. truit, always comb w. kolaṭṭhi\-mattiyo, of boils AN.v.170 = Snp.p.125, cp SN.i.150;
* *\-[rukkha](rukkha.md)* the j. tree Snp\-a.356; DN\-a.i.262;
* *\-[sampāka](sampāka.md)* cooked with (the juice of) jujube Vv.43#5 (= Vv\-a.186).
