at Vin.ii.131 is doubtful reading (see p. Vin.ii.318, variant reading uḍḍhetvā), and should perhaps be read *uḍḍetvā* (oḍḍetva, see *[uḍḍeti](uḍḍeti.md)*), meaning “putting into a sling, tying or binding up”.
