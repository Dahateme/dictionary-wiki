having no hunger, being without cravings, stilled, satisfied. Epithet of an Arahant always in combination with *[nibbuta](nibbuta.md)* or *[parinibbuta](parinibbuta.md)*: SN.iii.26 (tanhaṃ abbuyha); SN.iv.204 (vedanānaṃ khayā); MN.i.341; MN.i.412, AN.iv.410; AN.v.65 (sītibhūta); Snp.707 (aniccha), Snp.735, Snp.758; Iti.48 (esanānaṃ khayā); Thig.132 (abbūḷhasalla)  
■ Explained at Pts.ii.243 by nekkhammena kāmacchandato n.; arahattamaggena sabbakilesehi n. muccati.
