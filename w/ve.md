Ve1

indeclinable part. of affirmation, emphasizing the preceding word: indeed, truly Vin.i.3 (etaṃ ve sukhaṃ); Dhp.63 (*[sa](sa.md)* ve bāḷo ti vuccati), Dhp.83 (sabbattha ve), Dhp.163 (yaṃ ve… taṃ ve); Snp.1050 Snp.1075, Snp.1082; Dhp\-a.iii.155 (= yeva). See also *[have](have.md)*.

cp. Vedic vē, vai

Ve2

may be enclitic form of *[tumhe](tumhe.md)*, for the usual *[vo](vo.md)* at Snp.333 (= tumhākaṃ Snp\-a.339). See P.T.S. ed. of Sn; cp variant reading *[ve](ve.md)* for *[vo](vo.md)* at Snp.560 (here as particle!).

Ve˚

is the *guṇa* (increment) form of *vi˚*; found in many secondary (mostly f. & nt. abstr.) derivations from words with; *vi˚*; e.g. vekalla, vecikicchin, veneyya, vepulla vematta, vevicchā, veramaṇī, which Bdhgh explains simply as “vi\-kārassa ve\-kāraṃ katvā veramaṇī” Kp\-a.24. Cp. *[veyy˚](veyy˚.md)*.
