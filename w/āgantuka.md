# āgantuka

``` toml
dict_label = "NCPED"
word = "āgantuka"
```

1. (mfn.) added, accessory; incidental adventitious, external.
2. (mfn. and m.f) arriving, coming; a newcomer, a stranger; a traveller, esp. a visiting *bhikkhu* or bhikkhum.
