is the P. correspondent of sajati1 *(sṛj)*, but Sk. *sañj* = sajjati (to hang on, cling), which at Dhtp.67 Dhtp.397 defined as *[saṅga](saṅga.md)*. The Dhtp (64) & Dhtm (82) take; *sañj* in all meanings of *[āliṅgana](āliṅgana.md)* (= sajati2), *[vissagga](vissagga.md)* (= sajati1), & *[nimmāna](nimmāna.md)* (= sajjeti).
