to turn down or away (perhaps in dogmatic sense to turn away from saṃsāra), to divert destroy: only in phrase *vivaṭṭayi saṃyojanaṃ* (in standard setting with *acchecchi taṇhaṃ*), where the usual variant reading is *vāvattayi* (see *[vāvatteti](vāvatteti.md)*). Thus at MN.i.12 MN.i.122; SN.i.127; SN.iv.105, SN.iv.205, SN.iv.207, SN.iv.399; AN.i.134; AN.iii.246 AN.iii.444 sq.; AN.iv.8 sq.; Iti.47 (T. vivattayi).
