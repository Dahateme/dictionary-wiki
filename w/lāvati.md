Lāvati & Lāveti

to cut, to mow Pv\-a.40 (lāvitvā), Mhvs.10, Mhvs.30 (lāvayati).

the latter the usual form, as Caus. of lunāti. lāvati is the simple Pāli formation fr. *lū* Another Caus. ii. is *lavāpati* (q.v.). See also *[lāyati](lāyati.md)*.
