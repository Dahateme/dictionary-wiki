indeclinable

1. part of comparison: like, as; stands for iva (usually in *verse* after *[ā](ā.md)*: Snp.420 (jātimā v.); Pv.i.8#5 (vārinā v.); or *[o](o.md)* Snp.580 (vajjho v.), Snp.818 (kapaṇo v.); or ṃ: Snp.381 (vajantaṃ v.), Snp.689 (nekkhaṃ v.).
2. dubitative particle: *na viya maññe* I suppose not MN.ii.121. Cp. *[byā](byā.md)*.

another form of *[iva](iva.md)*, *viâ*, *\*via* (so some Prākrits: Pischel *Prk. Gr.*, § 143, 336) → viya. Pischel *Prk. Gr.* § 336, 337 derives it fr. viva = v’ iva

Viya˚

the diaeretic form (for sake of metre) of *vya˚*;, which see generally. Cp. the identical *veyya˚*.

= vi \+ a˚
