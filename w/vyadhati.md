to tremble, shake, waver; to be frightened Vin.ii.202 (so for *[vyādhati](vyādhati.md)*); Ja.iii.398 (vyadhase; C vyadhasi = kampasi)  
caus *vyadheti (& vyādheti);* to frighten, confuse Ja.iv.166 (= vyādheti bādheti C.)\-Fut. *vyādhayissati* SN.i.120 = Thag.46 (by˚). Under *[byādheti](byādheti.md)* we had given a different derivation (viz Caus. fr. *[vyādhi](vyādhi.md)*).

in poetry for the usual *[vedhati](vedhati.md)* of *vyath*, cp. Goth. wipōn
