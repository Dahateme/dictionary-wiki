Tasati1

to be thirsty fig. to crave for SN.ii.13; Mil.254  
pp tasita1. Cp pari˚.

Sk. tṛṣyati = Gr. τέρσομοι to dry up, Lat. torreo (= E. torrid, toast), Goth. gapairsan & gapaúrsnan Ohg. derren; see also *[taṇhā](taṇhā.md)* & taṇhīyati

Tasati2

to tremble, shake, to have fear; to be frightened Snp.394 (ye thāvarā ye ca tasanti loke) Cnd.479 (= santāsaṃ āpajjati); Kp\-a.245 (may be taken as tasati1, see *[tasa](tasa.md)*)  
pp *tasita2*, cp. also tasa uttasati.

Vedic trasati = Gr. τρέω, Lat. terreo (= terror); *\*ters* fr. *\*ter* in Sk. tarala, cp. also Lat. tremo (= tremble) and trepidus
