# nikkhipati

``` toml
dict_label = "NCPED"
word = "nikkhipati"
summary = "throws or puts or lays down; puts into; places, se"
grammar_comment = "pr. 3 sg."
```

1. throws or puts or lays down; puts into; places, sets down; sets out; lays (eggs)
2. lays aside; sets aside; puts away, stores; deposits; entrusts.

3. to eliminate, get rid of, give up Pv.ii.6#15 (dehaṃ to get rid of the body) Dhs\-a.344 (vitthāra\-desanaṃ).
