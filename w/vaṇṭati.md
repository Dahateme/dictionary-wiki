to partition, share; is given as root *vaṇṭ*; at Dhtp.92, Dhtp.561 and Dhtm.787 in meaning “vibhājana.”\-Another root *vaṇṭ*; is found at Dhtm.108 with unmeaning expln “vaṇṭ’ atthe.”
