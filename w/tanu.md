* *\-[karaṇa](karaṇa.md)* making thinner, reducing, diminishing Vin.ii.316 (Bdhgh on CV. v.9, 2);
* *\-[bhāva](bhāva.md)* decrease Pp.17;
* *\-[bhūta](bhūta.md)* decreased, diminished Pp.17; esp in phrase *˚soka* with diminished grief, having one’s grief allayed Dhp\-a.iii.176; Pv\-a.38.
