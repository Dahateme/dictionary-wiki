1. name and form;
   1. individual person or identity; interpreted as the mental and physical aspects of an individual.
   2. esp. in the *paṭiccasamuppāda* series: with *viññāṇa* furnishing (the possibility of) individual identity (conditioned by *avijjā* and *saṅkhārā*) and experience (by means of the senses)
