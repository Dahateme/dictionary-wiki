setting forth, putting forward; only in cpd. *sati˚*; setting up of mindfulness (q.v. and see discussion of term at *Dial* ii.324) Besides in later lit. meaning “origin,” starting point cause, in title of the 7th book of the Abhidhamma, also called Mahāpakaraṇa. See Ledi, *J.P.T.S.* 1915–⁠16 p. 26; Mrs. Rh. D., Tika p. 1, vi
