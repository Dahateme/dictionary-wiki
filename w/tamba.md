copper (“the dark metal”); usually in combinations, signifying colour of or made of (cp. loha bronze), e.g. lākhātamba (adj.) Thig.440 (colour of an ox); *\-akkhin* Vv.32#3 (timira˚) Sdhp.286; *\-nakhin* Ja.vi.290; *\-nettā* (f.) ibid.; *\-bhājana* Dhp\-a.i.395; *\-mattika* Dhp\-a.iv.106; *\-vammika* Dhp\-a.iii.208; *\-loha* Pv\-a.95 (= loha).

Sk. tāmra, orig. adj. = dark coloured, leaden; cp. Sk. adj. taṃsra id., to tama
