at Ja.ii.448 is doubtful, variant reading *[okkantika](okkantika.md)*. It is used adverbially: *okkandikaṃ kīḷati* to sport (loudly or joyfully). C. explains as “migo viya okkandi\-katvā kīḷati”; in the way of roaring(?) or frisking about(?), like a deer.
