adjective eastern i.e. facing the (rising) sun (opp. pacchā) Ja.i.50 (˚sīsaka, of Māyādevī’s couch), Ja.i.212 (˚lokadhātu) Mil.6; DN\-a.i.311 (˚mukha facing east); Dhp\-a.iii.155 (id.); Vv\-a.190; Pv\-a.74, Pv\-a.256. The opposite *[apācīna](apācīna.md)* (e.g. SN.iii.84) is only apparently a neg. pācīna, in reality a der. fr. *apa* (apa \+ *ac*), as pācīna is a der. fr pra \+ *ac*. See apācīna.

Vedic prācīna, fr. adv. prāc bent forward
