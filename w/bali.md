2. tax, revenue (cp. Zimmer *Altind. Leben* 166 & Fick, *Sociale Gliederung* 75; DN.i.135, DN.i.142; Ja.i.199 (daṇḍa˚ fines & taxes), Ja.i.339; Dhp\-a.i.251 (daṇḍa˚).
3. Np. of an Asura DN.ii.259.
