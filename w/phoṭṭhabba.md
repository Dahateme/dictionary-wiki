tangible, touch, contact; it is synonymous with phassa, which it replaces in psychol. terminology. *Phoṭṭhabbaṃ* is the senseobject of *[kāya](kāya.md)* (or *[taca](taca.md)*) touch (“kāyena phoṭṭhabbaṃ phusitvā” DN.iii.226, DN.iii.250, DN.iii.269; Cnd.p.238 under rūpa) See also *[āyatana](āyatana.md)*  
■ DN.iii.102 (in list of ajjhattikabāhirāni āyatanāni: kāyo c’ eva phoṭṭhabbā ca; with pl. like m.); Vb\-a.79 (˚dhātu).

grd. of phusati
