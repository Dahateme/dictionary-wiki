adverb “with the same steps,” i.e. without interruption, constant successive (cp. Lat stante pede & Sk. adv.; *[sapadi](sapadi.md)* at once).

1. lit. (perhaps a later use) of a *bird* at Ja.v.358 (s. sāliṃ khādanto, without a stop); of a *lion* at Mil.400 (sapadāna\-bhakkha).
2. applied in phrase *sapadānaṃ carati* to go on uninterrupted alms\-begging Vin.iv.191; SN.iii.238; Snp.413; Ja.i.66; Pv.iv.3#44; Vv\-a.121; and in phrases *sapadāna\-cārikā* Ja.i.89; *\-cārika* (adj.)
