adjective with beautiful banks. Usually spelt *su˚*; as if su \+ patittha (see *[patittha](patittha.md)*), e.g. Vin.iii.108; Ja.vi.518, Ja.vi.555 (= sobhana˚); DN.ii.129; Ud.83; Pv.ii.1#20 (= sundara\-tittha Pv\-a.77). But *sū˚*; at MN.i.76, MN.i.283 Tha\-ap.333.

su \+ upatittha, the latter = tittha, cp. upavana: vana
