Ohīyati & ohiyyati
1. to be left behind, to stay behind Ja.v.340 (avahīyati ohiyyati C.).
2. to stay behind, to fall out (in order to urinate or defecate); ger. *[ohīyitvā](ohīyitvā.md)* Vin.iv.229; Dhp\-a.ii.21 (cp. ohanati). See also *[ohiyyaka](ohiyyaka.md)*.
