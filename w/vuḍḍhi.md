feminine increase, growth, furtherance, prosperity.

1. *[vuḍḍhi](vuḍḍhi.md)* Pv\-a.22 Often in phrase *vuḍḍhi virūḷhi vepulla* (all three almost tautological) Mil.51; Vism.129.
