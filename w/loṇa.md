salt; as adj., salty, of salt, alkaline\- Vin.i.202 (loṇāni bhesajjāni alkaline medicine among which are given sāmuddaṃ kāḷaloṇaṃ sindhavaṃ ubbhidaṃ bilaṃ as var. kinds of salt), Vin.i.220 = Vin.i.243 (as flavouring, with tela, taṇḍula & khādaniya); AN.i.210 AN.i.250; AN.iv.108; Mil.63; Dhp\-a.iv.176 (in simile see below) Vv\-a.98, Vv\-a.100, Vv\-a.184 (aloṇa sukkha\-kummāsa, unsalted) On loṇa in similes cp. *J.P.T.S.* 1907, 131.

* *\-[ambila](ambila.md)* acid and salt Ja.i.505; Ja.ii.171, Ja.ii.394.
* *\-[odaka](odaka.md)* salt water Ja.vi.37; Vv\-a.99 (˚udaka).
* *\-[kāra](kāra.md)* salt\-maker Vin.i.350 (˚gāma); AN.ii.182 (˚dāraka); Ja.vi.206 (kara) Mil.331.
* *\-[ghaṭa](ghaṭa.md)* a pitcher with salt SN.ii.276. See also App. to Kp\-a.68 (in Sn Index 870, 871) on Vism passage with loṇaghaṭaka.
* *\-[dhūpana](dhūpana.md)* salt\-spicing Vb\-a.311 (viya sabba vyañjanesu; i.e. the strongest among all flavourings).
* *\-[phala](phala.md)* a crystal of (natural salt [phala for phaṭa = \*sphaṭa, cp. phalaka] AN.i.250 (in simile).
* *\-[rasa](rasa.md)* alkaline taste AN.iv.199, AN.iv.203.
* *\-[sakkhara](sakkhara.md)* a salt crystal (cp. ˚phala), a (solid) piece of (natural) salt SN.ii.276 (in simile, cp. AN.i.250); Snp\-a.222 (aggimhi pakkhitta l\-s., in the same simile at Dhp\-a.iv.176: uddhane pakkhitta\-loṇa).
* *\-[sakkharikā](sakkharikā.md)* a piece of salt\-crystal, used as a caustic for healing wounds Vin.i.206.
* *\-[sovīraka](sovīraka.md)* salted sour gruel Vin.i.210; Vv\-a.99. Lonika & Loniya;

cp. Sk. lavaṇa, for which see also *[lavaṇa](lavaṇa.md)*. The Prk. form is loṇa
