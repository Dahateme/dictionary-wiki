indeclinable prohibition particle: not, do not, let us hope not, I wish that… not [cp Lat. utinam & ne]. Constructed with various tenses e.g.

1. with *aor.* (prohibitive tense): mā evaṃ *akattha* do not thus Dhp\-a.i.7; mā *abhaṇi* speak not Pv.i.3#3; mā *cintayittha* do not worry Dhp\-a.i.12; mā *[parihāyi](parihāyi.md)* I hope he will not go short (or be deprived) of… MN.i.444 mā *[bhāyi](bhāyi.md)* fear not Ja.ii.159; mā *mariṃsu* I hope they will not die Ja.iii.55; mā (te) *[rucci](rucci.md)* may it not please (you), i.e. please do not Vin.ii.198; mā evaṃ *ruccittha* id. Dhp\-a.i.13.
2. with *imper.:* mā *[gaccha](gaccha.md)* Ja.i.152 mā *detha* Ja.iii.275. mā *[ghāta](ghāta.md)* do not kill: see *[māghāta](māghāta.md)*
3. with *pot.:* mā *anuyuñjetha* Dhp.27; mā *bhuñjetha* let him not eat Mhvs.25, Mhvs.113; mā *vadetha* Ja.vi.364.
4. with *indic. pres.:* mā *[paṭilabhati](paṭilabhati.md)* AN.v.194  
   ■ A peculiar use is found in phrase *ānemi mā ānemi* shall I bring it *or not?* Ja.vi.334.
5. mā = na (simple negation in māsakkhimhā we could not Vin.iii.23. \-Ma

cp. Vedic mā, Gr. μή
