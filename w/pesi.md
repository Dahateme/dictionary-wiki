Pesi (pesī)

feminine

1. a lump, usually a mass of flesh Ja.iii.223 = Dhp\-a.iv.67 (pesi = maṃsapesi C.). Thus *[maṃsapesi](maṃsapesi.md)*, muscle Vin.ii.25 ≈ (maṃsapes ûpamā kāmā); Vin.iii.105; MN.i.143, MN.i.364; SN.ii.256; SN.iv.193 (in characteristic of lohitaka); Vism.356; Pv\-a.199.
2. the foetus in the third stage after conception (between *abbuda & ghana;*) SN.i.206; Ja.iv.496; Mnd.120; Mil.40 Vism.236.
3. a piece, bit (for pesikā), in *veḷu˚* Ja.iv.205.

cp. Epic Sk. peśī
