only at SN.v.421; cp. SN.iv.330 (Dhammacakka\-p\-Sutta). It is a misreading. Read with Oldenberg Vin.i.10, *kāmesu kāmasukhallikānuyoga* (devotion to the passions, to the pleasures of sense). See *[kāmasukha](kāmasukha.md)* and *[allika](allika.md)*.
