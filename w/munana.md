fathoming, recognising, knowing; a C. word to explain “muni,” used by Dhpāla at Vv\-a.114 (mahā\-isibhūtaṃ… mahanten’ eva ñāṇena munanato paricchindanato mahā muniṃ), & Vv\-a.231 (anavasesassa ñeyyassa munanato muni).

fr. munāti, almost equal to mona
