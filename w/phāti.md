swelling, increase Ja.ii.426 (= vaḍḍhi); Vism.271 (vuddhi \+). Usually combined with *kṛ*; as *phāti\-kamma* increase, profit, advantage Vin.ii.174 Vb\-a.334 & *phāti\-karoti* to make fat, to increase, to use to advantage MN.i.220 = AN.v.347; AN.iii.432.

cp. Sk. sphāti, fr. *sphāy*, sphāyate to swell, increase (Idg. \*spē(i), as in Lat. spatium, Ohg. spuot Ags. spēd = E. speed; see Walde, *Lat. Wtb.* s. v. spatium) pp. sphīta = P. phīta
