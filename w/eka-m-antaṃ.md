eka\-m\-antaṃ

adv. (or ekam antaṃ)

1. to one side (showing respect); at a respectful distance.
2. on one side, aside; out of the way; a little apart.
3. to one side, apart; at a distance; in private, on one’s own.
4. in one place; in a single place
