weariness, disgust with worldly life, tedium, aversion, indifference, disenchantment N. is of the preliminary & conditional states for the attainment of Nibbāna (see *[nibbāna](nibbāna.md)* II B 1) & occurs frequently together with; *virāga, vimutti & nibbāna;* in the formula: etaṃ ekanta\-nibbidāya virāgāya nirodhāya… sambodhāya nibbānāya saṃvattati “this leads to being thoroughly tired (of the world), to dispassionateness to destruction (of egoism), to perfect wisdom, to Nibbāna,” e.g. at DN.i.189; SN.v.82, SN.v.179, SN.v.255 SN.v.361; AN.iii.83; AN.iv.143; AN.v.216
