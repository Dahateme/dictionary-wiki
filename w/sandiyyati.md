*sandīyati* to be vexed, to resent SN.ii.200 sq.; Ja.vi.570 (spelt wrongly sandhīyati C. explains as “manku hoti”).

saṃ \+ diyyati(= dīyati) = Sk. dīyate of dyati, i.e. *dā2* to cut: see *[dātta](dātta.md)*
