# āsava

``` toml
dict_label = "NCPED"
word = "āsava"
summary = "‘inflowing’, influence (the concerns, attitudes, p"
grammar_comment = "masculine & neuter"
```

1. ‘inflowing’, influence (the concerns, attitudes, predispositions, listed as kāma, bhava, avijjā, and sometimes, ditthi, which form an obstacle to the realization of the truth.
2. affliction, pain (so commentary).
3. discharge (from a awound); of things, and so, in themselves and through the actions they motivate, bring about further existence; their ending or destruction is in fact arahatship).

that which; flows (out or on to) outflow & influx.

1. spirit, the intoxicating extract or secretion of a tree or flower, O. C in Vin.iv.110 (four kinds); B. on DN.iii.182 (five kinds Dhs\-a.48; Kp\-a.26; Ja.iv.222; Ja.vi.9.

3. in psychology t.t. for certain specified ideas which intoxicate the mind (bemuddle it, befoozle it, so that it cannot rise to higher things).
