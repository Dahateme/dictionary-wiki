Vīti˚

is the contracted prepositional combination *vi \+ ati*, representing an emphatic *[ati](ati.md)*, e.g. in the foll.:

* *\-(k)kama* 1 going beyond, transgression, sin Vin.iii.112; Vin.iv.290; Ja.i.412; Ja.iv.376; Pp.21; Mil.380 Vism.11, Vism.17; Dhp\-a.iv.3 2 going on, course (of time) Pv\-a.137 (˚ena by and by; variant reading anukkamena)
* *\-[kiṇṇa](kiṇṇa.md)* sprinkled, speckled, gay with Ja.v.188.
* *\-[nāmeti](nāmeti.md)* to make pass (time), to spend the time, to live, pass wait Ja.iii.63, Ja.iii.381; Dhp\-a.ii.57; Vv\-a.158; Pv\-a.12, Pv\-a.21 Pv\-a.47, Pv\-a.76.
* *\-[patati](patati.md)* to fly past, to flit by, to fly up & down Snp.688; AN.v.88 = Mil.392.
* *\-[missa](missa.md)* mingled, mixed (with) MN.i.318; DN.iii.96; Ja.vi.151.
* *\-[vatta](vatta.md)* having passed or overcome, gone through; passed, spent SN.i.14, SN.i.145; SN.iii.225; SN.iv.52; AN.ii.44; Snp.6, Snp.395, Snp.796; Ja.i.374; Thag\-a.170; Pv\-a.21, Pv\-a.55, Pv\-a.83.
* *\-[sāreti](sāreti.md)* DN.i.52, DN.i.90, DN.i.118, DN.i.152; Snp.419; cp. Mil.19; Ja.iv.98 (shortened to sārāṇīyaṃ vītisārimha; explained with sārayimha); Ja.v.264.
* *\-[haraṇa](haraṇa.md)* passing (mutually), carrying in between Ja.vi.355 (bhojanānaṃ).
* *\-[harati](harati.md)* to associate with (at a meal) SN.i.162
* *\-[hāra](hāra.md)*, in *pada˚*; “taking over or exchange of steps,” a stride SN.i.211; AN.iv.429; Ja.vi.354. Same in BSk. e.g. Mvu.i.35; Mvu.iii.162.

fr. vi \+ ati *sṛ*; not with Childers fr. *smṛ*; cp. BSk. vyatisārayati to make pass (between), to exchange (greeting), to address, converse (kathaṃ), greet.
