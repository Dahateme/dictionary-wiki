Pacinati & Pacināti
1. to pick, pluck, gather, take up, collect, accumulate SN.iii.89 SN.iv.74 (dukkhaṃ = ācināti p. 73); Dhp.47, Dhp.48 (pupphāni ocinati Dhp\-a.i.366); Ja.iii.22; fut. pacinissati Dhp\-a.i.361.
2. to pick out (mentally), to discern, distinguish, realise, know Snp.837 (ppr. pacinaṃ = pacinanto vicinanto tulayanto tīrayanto Mnd.185; = pavicinati Snp\-a.545); fut. *[pacessati](pacessati.md)* Dhp.44, Dhp.45 (sic F.; MSS vijessati, & *vicessati* the latter perhaps preferable to pac˚; explained at Dhp\-a.i.334 by vicinissati upaparikkhissati paṭivijjhissati sacchikarissati)

pass *pacīyati* to be heaped up, to increase, accumulate SN.iv.74 (opp. khīyati).
