adjective

1. with Pajāpati. The passage under *[pajāpati](pajāpati.md)* 1 was distorted through copyist’s default. It should read: “only in one formula, with Inda Brahmā, viz. devā sa\-indakā sa\-brahmakā sa\-pajāpatikā DN.ii.274 (without sa\-brahmakā); SN.iii.90; AN.v.325. Otherwise *[sapajāpatika](sapajāpatika.md)* in sense of foll. Also at Vb\-a.497 with Brahmā.”
2. with one’s wife Vin.i.23; Vin.iv.62; Ja.i.345.
