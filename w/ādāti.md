Ādāti (Ādadāti)

to take up, accept, appropriate, grasp, seize;  
grd *[ādātabba](ādātabba.md)* Vin.i.50;  
inf *ādātuṃ* DN.iii.133 (adinnaṃ theyyasankhātaṃ ā.).  
ger *ādā & ādāya;* (see sep.);  
grd *[ādeyya](ādeyya.md)*,  
caus *ādapeti* (q.v.)
