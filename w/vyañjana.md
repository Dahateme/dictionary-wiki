2. letter (of a word) as opposed to *[attha](attha.md)* (meaning sense, spirit), e, g. DN.iii.127; SN.iv.281, SN.iv.296; SN.v.430; AN.ii.139 (Cp. *[savyañjana](savyañjana.md)*); or *[pada](pada.md)* (word), e.g. MN.i.213; AN.i.59; AN.ii.147, AN.ii.168, AN.ii.182; AN.iii.178 sq.; Vin.ii.316; Ne.4; Snp\-a.177
