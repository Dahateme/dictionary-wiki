crab, in kulīra\- *[pādaka](pādaka.md)* “a crab\-footer,” i.e. a (sort of) bedstead Vin.ii.149; Vin.iv.40 (kulira), cp. Bdhgh on latter passage at Vin.iv.357 (kuḷira˚ and kuḷiya˚): a bedstead with curved or carved legs; esp. when carved to represent animal’s feet (*Vin. Texts* iii.164).
