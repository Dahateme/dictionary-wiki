= muṇḍa; cp. BSk. muṇḍaka Divy.13  
■ Snp.p.80; Dhp.264 (= sīsa\-muṇḍana\-matta Dhp\-a.iii.391 qualification of a shaveling); Vv\-a.67 (˚samaṇā, Dvandva)  
■ *aḍḍha˚*; shaven over one half the head (sign of loss of freedom) Mhvs.6, Mhvs.42  
■ *kaṇṇa˚*; “with blunt corners,” Name of one of the 7 great lakes: see under *[kaṇṇa](kaṇṇa.md)*. *\-[paṭisīsaka](paṭisīsaka.md)* the chignon of a shaveling, in phrase: kāsāyaṃ nivāsetvā *muṇḍaka\-paṭisīsakaṃ* sīse paṭimuñcitvā fastening the (imitation) top\-knot of a shaveling to his head Mil.90; cp. Ja.ii.197 (paccekabuddha\-vesaṃ gaṇhitvā paṭisīsakaṃ paṭimuñcitvā) similarly Ja.v.49.
