distorted fr. Vedic puraṃ\-dara, pura \+ *dṛ*; to break, see *[darī](darī.md)*, thus “breaker of fortresses,” epithet of Indra (& Agni). The P. Commentator (Vv\-a.171) of course takes it popularly as “pure dānaṃ dadātī ti Purindado ti vuccati,” thus pure \+ *dā*; see also Trenckner, *Notes* 596; Geiger,
