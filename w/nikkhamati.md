# nikkhamati

``` toml
dict_label = "NCPED"

grammar_comment = "pr. 3 sg."
```

goes or comes out (esp. out of a town or village); comes forth (to fight etc); emerges; gets out; escapes; leaves; esp. leaves the household life; sticks out, protrudes.
