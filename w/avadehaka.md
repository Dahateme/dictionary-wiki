(\-˚) adjective in the idiom *udarāvadehakaṃ bhuñjati*, to eat one’s fill MN.i.102; Thag.935. Vism.33 has udarāvadehaka\-bhojana, a heavy meal.

ava \+ deha \+ ka but more likely direct fr. ava \+ *dih*
