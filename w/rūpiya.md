* *\-[maya](maya.md)* made of silver Vin.ii.112; SN.iii.144 (sovaṇṇamaya \+); Pv.ii.6#4 (where in sequence sovaṇṇa˚, maṇi˚ loha˚ r.; explained as “rajatamaya” Pv\-a.95); Dhp\-a.i.29.
