* *\-[kāra](kāra.md)* the making of a hut, in *˚sikkhāpada*, a rule regarding the method of building a hut Ja.ii.282; Ja.iii.78 Ja.iii.351;
* *\-[dūsaka](dūsaka.md)* destroying a hut or nest Dhp\-a.ii.23
* *\-purisa* a “hut man,” a peasant Mil.147.
