adjective belonging to ordinary man, common, ordinary, in 2 combinations viz.

1. phrase *hīna gamma p. anariya* Vin.i.10; SN.iv.330; AN.v.216
2. with ref. to *[iddhi](iddhi.md)* Vin.ii.183; Ja.i.360; Vism.97. Cp. *Vin. Texts* iii.230. The BSk. forms are either pārthag\-janika Lal.540, or prāthug\-janika Mvu.iii.331.

fr. puthujjana
