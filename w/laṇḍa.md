excrement, dung of animals, dirt; mostly used with ref to elephants (haṭṭhi˚), e.g. at Ja.ii.19; Dhp\-a.i.163, Dhp\-a.i.192 Dhp\-a.iv.156 (here also as *assa˚*; horse dung.) Cp. *[laṇḍikā](laṇḍikā.md)*.

cp. Sk. laṇḍa (dial.). The Dhtm under No. 155 gives a root *laḍ* in meaning “jigucchana,” i.e. disgust.
