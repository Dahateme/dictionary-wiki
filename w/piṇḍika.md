(\-˚) in *chatta˚\-vivara* is a little doubtful, the phrase prob. means “a crevice in the covering (i.e. the round mass) of the canopy or sunshade” Ja.vi.376. Dutoit (*J. translation* vi.457) translates “opening at the back of the sunshade,” thus evidently reading “*[piṭṭhika](piṭṭhika.md)*.”
