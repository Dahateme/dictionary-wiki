1. *vind*: prs. nibbindati etc. usually in combination with *virajjati & vimuccati;* (cp nibbāna III. 2). 2. *vid*: Pot. *nibbide* (variant reading BB nibbije) Ja.v.368 (= nibbindeyya Com.); ger *[nibbijjitvā](nibbijjitvā.md)* Ja.i.82, & *[nibbijja](nibbijja.md)* Snp.448 = SN.i.124 (nibbijjâpema = nibbijja pakkameyya Snp\-a.393)

pp *[nibbiṇṇa](nibbiṇṇa.md)*. See also *[nibbidā](nibbidā.md)*.
