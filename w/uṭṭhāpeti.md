1. to make rise, only in phrase *aruṇaṃ* (suriyaṃ) *[u](u.md)*. to let the sun rise, i.e. wait for sunrise or to go on till sunrise Ja.i.318; Ja.vi.330 Vism.71, Vism.73 (aruṇaṃ).
2. to raise Ja.vi.32 (paṭhaviṃ).
3. to fit up Ja.vi.445 (nāvaṃ).
4. to exalt, praise DN\-a.i.256.
5. to turn a person out Dhp\-a.iv.69

■ See also *[vuṭṭhāpeti](vuṭṭhāpeti.md)*.
