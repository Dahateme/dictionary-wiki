(rohiccasarabhā migā = rohitā sarabhamigā, C. ibid. 538) Sarabhamigajātaka the 483rd Jātaka Ja.i.193, Ja.i.406 (text sarabhanga); Ja.iv.263 sq.

* *\-[pallaṅka](pallaṅka.md)* “antelope\-couch,” a high seat, from which the Bodhisat preaches Ja.iii.342 (cp. vara\-pallanka Ja.iii.364).
* *\-[pādaka](pādaka.md)* having legs like those of a gazelle Ja.i.267.

Vedic śarabha a sort of deer Ja.iv.267; Ja.vi.537
