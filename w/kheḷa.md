Avs.i.279 (kheṭamūtropajīvinī; Avs.ii.113: kheṭavadutsṛjya); Pv\-a.80 (= niṭṭhubhana).

* *\-[kilinna](kilinna.md)* wet with exudation Ja.i.164;
* *\-[mallaka](mallaka.md)* a spitting box, a cuspidor Vin.i.48; Vin.ii.175, Vin.ii.209 sq.
* *\-siṅghānikā* phlegm & mucus Dhp\-a.i.50.
