(usually \-˚) a heap, pile, mass, multitude Vin.ii.211; Ja.i.146 (sabba\-rogānaṃ). As in foll. compounds: *[aṭṭhi˚](aṭṭhi˚.md)* Iti.17 (\+ aṭṭhikandala); *kaṭṭha˚* AN.iii.408; AN.iv.72; Ja.ii.327; *gūtha˚* Ja.ii.211; *tiṇa˚* AN.iii.408; *palāla˚* DN.i.71; MN.iii.3; AN.i.241; AN.ii.210 *maṃsa˚* DN.i.52; *vālika˚* Ja.vi.560; *saṅkhāra˚* SN.i.135.

* *\-[kata](kata.md)* (& ˚kita) for *puñjikata*; cf. Sk. puñjīkṛta with i for a in compound with *kṛ & bhū* heaped up, heaped together Vin.ii.208 (puñjakita); MN.i.58, MN.i.89 (id. but id p. MN.iii.92 puñjakajāta); AN.iii.324 (puñjakata; variant reading puñjakita & puñjanika); Ja.ii.408 (puñjakata, variant reading pancalikata); Ja.vi.111 (id., variant reading puñca˚).
