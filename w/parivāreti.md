to cover, encompass, surround Ja.i.181 (nagaraṃ ˚ayiṃsu); Ja.ii.102 (fut. ˚essati), Ja.iii.371 (rukkhaṃ); Ja.iv.405 (for parikaroti); Ja.vi.179. ger. *[parivāretvā](parivāretvā.md)* used as prep. “round” Ja.i.172 (pokkharaṇiṃ)  
■ In meaning “to serve, attend upon,” also “to attend upon oneself, to amuse oneself,” *[parivāreti](parivāreti.md)* is often erroneously read for paricāreti, e.g. at DN.ii.13; Pv.iv.1#29 (variant reading ˚cāreti); Pv\-a.228; in ppr. med. *\-vāriyamāna* (with variant reading ˚cāriyamāna) at DN.ii.21; AN.i.145; Ja.i.58; Vv\-a.92  
■ See also *[anuparivāreti](anuparivāreti.md)*  
pp *[parivārita](parivārita.md)* (q.v.).

Caus. of pari \+ *vṛ*.
