brother, nom. sg. bhātā Snp.296; Ja.i.307; Pv\-a.54, Pv\-a.64 gen. sg. *bhātuno* Thag\-a.71 (Tha\-ap.36), & *bhātussa* Mhvs.8, Mhvs.9; instr. *bhātarā* Ja.i.308; acc. *bhātaraṃ* Snp.125; Ja.i.307; loc. *bhātari* Ja.iii.56
