to adorn oneself with (acc.), to fondle, treasure, take pride in (gen.) MN.i.260 (allīyati kelāyati dhanāyati mamāyati, where dhanāyati is to be read as vanāyati as shown by variant reading SN.iii.190 & MN.i.552); SN.iii.190 (id.) Mil.73  
pp *[keḷāyita](keḷāyita.md)*.

Denom. fr. *kīḷ* in meaning “to amuse oneself with,” i.e. take a pride in. Always combined with *[mamāyati](mamāyati.md)*. BSk. same meaning (to be fond of) śālikṣetrāṇi k. gopāyati Divy.631. Morris. *J.P.T.S.* 1893, 16 puts it (wrongly?) to *kel* to quiver: see also *[keḷanā](keḷanā.md)*
