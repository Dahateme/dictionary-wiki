to go, walk, waver; found only in Dhtp (No. 29) as root *vaṅg* in meaning “gamana. Perhaps confused with *valg*; see *[vaggati](vaggati.md)*.

cp. \*Sk. vangati, to which belongs vañjula. Idg. *\*ṷag* to bend; cp. Lat. vagor to roam, vagus = vague Ohg. wankon to waver
