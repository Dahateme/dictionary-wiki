cemetery; only in phrase *kaṭasiṃ vaḍḍheti* “to increase the cemetery” referring to dying and being buried repeatedly in the course of numerous rebirths, explained by susāna & āḷāhana Thag\-a.291\-vaḍḍhenti kaṭasiṃ ghoraṃ ādiyanti punabbhavaṃ Vin.ii.296 = AN.ii.54 = Thag.456 (where ācinanti (?) for ādiy˚), Thag.575; Thig.502. Also in compounds *\-vaḍḍhana* Ja.i.146; Ud.72 = Ne.174; *\-vaḍḍhita* SN.ii.178 sq. Cnd.664.

prob. a contamination of kaṭa \+ sīva(thikā), charnel\-house, under influence of foll. *[va](va.md)* (ḍḍh˚), cp Sk. kaṭa (?) a corpse
