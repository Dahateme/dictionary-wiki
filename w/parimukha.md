Also in phrase ˚ṃ kārāpeti (of hair) Vin.ii.134 “to cut off (? the hair in front” (i.e. on the breast) *Vin. Texts* iii.138 where is quoted Bdhgh’s expln “ure loma\-saṃharaṇaṃ.”
