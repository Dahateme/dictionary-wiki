■ instr. *saddhāya* (used as adv.) in faith, by faith in (acc. or gen.) Vin.ii.289 (āyasmantānaṃ) Ja.v.176 (pabbajita); Pv\-a.49 (kammaphalaṃ s.); or shortened to *[saddhā](saddhā.md)* (\-pabbajita) MN.i.123; AN.i.24; Ja.i.130. The same phrase as *saddhāya pabbajita* at SN.i.120 is explained as “saddahitvā” by Bdhgh (see *K.S.* i.321), thus taking it as ger.
