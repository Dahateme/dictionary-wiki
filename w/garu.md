# garu

1. (mfn.) heavy, weighty; burdensome, difficult.
2. 1. (mfn.) highly prized; of great account; (to be) esteemed or venerated.
   2. (mfn.) any venerated person (parents, elder, etc); esp. a religious (preceptor) a teacher.
