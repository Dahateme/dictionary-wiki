1. to reach, to get, obtain; ger. *samadhiggayha* MN.i.506; MN.ii.25; SN.i.86; Iti.16.
2. to exceed, surpass, to overcome, to master Ja.vi.261 (pañhaṃ samadhiggahetvā). Often confounded with *samatigaṇhāti*.
