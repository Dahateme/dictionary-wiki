# anupariveṇiyaṃ

should be written anu pariveṇiyaṃ (“in every cell, cell by cell”), anu here functioning as prep. c. loc. (see *[anu](anu.md)* A Vin.i.80, Vin.i.106.

anu \+ pariveṇiyaṃ = loc. of pariveṇi
