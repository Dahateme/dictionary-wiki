death, as ending *this* (visible) existence, physical death, in a narrower meaning than *[kālakiriyā](kālakiriyā.md)*; dying, in compounds death  
■ The customary stock definition of maraṇa runs; yaṃ tesaṃ tesaṃ sattānaṃ tamhā tamhā satta\-nikāyā cuti cavanatā bhedo antaradhānaṃ maccu maraṇaṃ kālakiriyā, khandhānaṃ bhedo, kaḷebarassa nikkhepo MN.i.49; Mnd.123, Mnd.124 (adds “jīvit’ indriyass’ upacchedo”). Cp. similar defns of birth and old age under *[jāti](jāti.md)* and *[jarā](jarā.md)*
