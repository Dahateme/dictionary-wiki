adjective however much, whatever, as many (in correlation with *[ta˚](ta˚.md)*; or *[tattaka](tattaka.md)*) Ja.v.74 (= yāvant); Vism.184 (yattakaṃ ṭhānaṃ gaṇhāti… tattakaṃ…), Vism.293 (yattakā = yāvatā); DN\-a.i.118 (yattaka… tattaka as long as); Dhp\-a.ii.50 (˚ṃ kālaṃ as long), Dhp\-a.ii.128 Vb\-a.73 (yattakaṃ ṭhānaṃ… tattakaṃ), Vb\-a.391 (yattakāni kusala\-cittāni… tesaṃ *sabbesaṃ*); Vv\-a.175 (yattakāni… tāni as many… so many, i.e. whatever), Vv\-a.285 (yattakā āhuneyyā nāma… tesu *sabbesu*…)  
■ instr. *yattakena* as adv. “because on account of” Dhp\-a.iii.383, Dhp\-a.iii.393.

fr. yāvant, a late formation; cp. Trenckner, *Notes*, 80
